About

ImageFormatter is a simple, intuitive web application designed to help users easily format and optimize images for the web. Whether you're a developer, designer, or photographer, our tool provides quick and efficient solutions for all your image formatting needs, supporting a wide range of formats and editing options. Our mission is to make image formatting accessible and hassle-free for everyone.
